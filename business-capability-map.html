Strategic Insights

Build Smarter

Key principles for leveraging Salesforce and AI in your capability roadmap.

☁️

Maximize Salesforce OOTB First

Salesforce natively covers ~60% of CRM, Sales, Service, and Marketing capabilities. Leverage these before custom development to reduce TCO and accelerate time-to-value.

🤖

AI Amplifies, Doesn't Replace

AI capabilities like Einstein, Agentforce, and custom LLMs work best layered on top of structured Salesforce data. Clean CRM data is the prerequisite for effective AI.

🎯

Identify Your AI-First Opportunities

Repetitive, data-intensive processes — document processing, lead scoring, case routing — are highest-ROI targets for AI automation across every industry.

🔗

MuleSoft Bridges the Gap

For capabilities outside Salesforce's core (supply chain, HR, ERP), MuleSoft integration enables a unified data layer that makes AI across the enterprise possible.

⚡

Agentforce Changes the Service Model

Autonomous AI agents built on Salesforce can now handle L1-L2 service cases end-to-end, fundamentally changing how service capabilities are staffed and measured.

📊

Capability Maps Drive Buy vs. Build

A clear capability map with OOTB and AI classification directly informs your buy-build-partner decisions, saving months of architecture and procurement effort.
